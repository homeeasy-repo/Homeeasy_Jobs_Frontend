About Us

Home Easy is a real estate services company that leverages technology. Our use of proprietary AI technology enables us to analyze both online and offline data to pinpoint the most valuable apartments within Class A Multi-family properties. Additionally, we employ bots to handle routine tasks, allowing brokers in local partner firms to engage in more focused, knowledgeable, and dedicated client interactions. Operating as a technology and marketing services provider, we collaborate with real estate, mortgage, and insurance brokerages in IL, TX, CO, AZ, and GA.
