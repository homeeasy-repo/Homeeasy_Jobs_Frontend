Join Homeeasy and Shape the Future of Real Estate

Home Easy is a technology-enabled real estate services firm. We use proprietary AI technology to analyze online and offline data in order to identify the best value apartments in Class A Multi-family properties. The firm also uses bots to alleviate the burden of remedial work from brokers in local partner brokerage firms, freeing them up for the most focused, well-informed, and dedicated client interactions. The company operates as a technology and marketing services provider in tandem with real estate, mortgage, and insurance brokerages in IL, TX, CO, AZ, and GA.

  • Python Proficiency: Including major libraries (Numpy, Pandas, Scipy, etc.).
  • Framework Experience: Experience with Python back-end frameworks (Flask preferred).

  • Version Control: Experience with Git/Github.
  • REST API Understanding: Operations (GET, POST, PUT, DELETE).

  • Automation Tools: Experience with Zapier.
  • Additional Tools: Exposure to Heroku, Follow Up Boss, and Twilio.

Our Offer:

  • Competitive Salary: Negotiable based on experience and skill set.
  • Travel: Company-funded global travel as needed.
